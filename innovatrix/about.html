About Innovatrix.her

Building bridges, breaking barriers, and inspiring the next generation of innovators.

Who We Are

At Innovatrix.her, we believe diversity fuels discovery. Our mission is to create opportunities, mentorship, and a global community for women to thrive in STEM.

We are a passionate team of educators, engineers, and advocates dedicated to closing the gender gap in technology and science. Through our programs, we provide the resources and support for women to excel in their careers and become leaders in their fields.
